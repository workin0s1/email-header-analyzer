How to Identify Fake Emails Using Headers

Fake or scam emails often use spoofed addresses. Here's how you can detect them using email headers:

  • Check the “Received” lines to trace real sender IP
  • Verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC results
  • Compare “From” and “Return-Path” headers
